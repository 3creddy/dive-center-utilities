1. Purpose & Scope

This SOP defines the standards for DIVEIndia’s photo & video operations: camera care, battery/card rotation, storage on laptop/Google Drive, and storytelling capture for the guest experience — especially critical for Basic Diver.

2. Equipment Management

  • Camera assignment: Only trained staff operate underwater cameras
  • Batteries: Charge after every use; keep labeled spares
  • Memory cards: Copy → verify backup → then format; rotate to avoid overuse
  • Cleaning/Maintenance: Fresh‑water rinse, O‑ring inspection & silicone grease; check ports for scratches

4. Shooting Guidelines for BD

Objective: tell the guest’s first‑dive story from start to finish without disrupting safety or enjoyment.

  • Pre‑Dive: Portrait in gear; short clip of briefing or buddy check
  • Confined Practice: Mask clear; reg clear/recover; first breaths underwater
  • Dive Tour: Wide/context shots; guest with reef/fish backdrop; a couple of close‑ups
  • Post‑Dive: Surfacing; smiles; group shot if applicable
Best Practices: Maintain distance; avoid silting/reef contact; prioritize guest comfort; do not over‑direct the dive for shots.

5. Delivery Workflow

  1. Confirm guest’s mySSI profile photo is uploaded before dive (for recognition card)
  2. Post‑dive: transfer media → quick quality check
  3. Upload to Google Drive (or chosen method); share link to guest within 24 hours
  4. Record delivery in daily media log

6. Integration with BD SOP

  • Camera operator coordinates names/times with BD instructor
  • Ensure mySSI logging is completed before media delivery
  • Front desk confirms billing completion before or alongside media delivery
