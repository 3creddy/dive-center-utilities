1. Purpose & Scope

This SOP defines DIVEIndia’s operational standards for delivering the Basic Diver (BD) introductory experience under SSI guidelines. It ensures consistent guest experience, safety, legal compliance, and integration with related processes (billing, paperwork, and media capture).

2. Program Overview

ItemStandard
TargetFirst‑time divers, non‑certified guests
OutcomeBasic Diver recognition in mySSI; credit toward further training
Duration~3 hours (briefing + confined skills + dive)
DepthMax 12 m (or shallower per standards/conditions)
RatiosPer SSI standards and conditions (e.g., 1:1–1:2)

3. Prerequisites

  • Minimum age per SSI standards
  • Medical Declaration; doctor’s clearance if required
  • Responsible Diver Code signed
  • Liability Waiver signed
  • mySSI account created with profile photo uploaded

4. Paperwork & Digital Checks

  1. mySSI account created before arrival or at check‑in; profile photo uploaded
  2. Unlock Basic Diver program for guest in mySSI (staff)
  3. Verify forms completed and valid:
    • Liability Release
    • Medical Statement
    • Responsible Diver Code
  4. Billing workflow: 50% deposit at booking; 50% balance after BD
  5. Instructor/Guide confirms all paperwork before water entry
Important: No guest may enter the water until paperwork is verified and the BD course is unlocked in mySSI.

5. Pre‑Dive Briefing & Skills

  • Gear introduction: mask, fins, BCD, regulator, gauges/computer
  • Hand signals and equalization techniques
  • Core skills: mask clearing, regulator recovery/clearing, BCD inflate/deflate
  • Breathing practice at surface; emphasis on relaxed pace and comfort

6. Dive Execution

  • Confined water practice in a shallow, calm area (e.g., Nemo Reef)
  • Guided tour within standards (depth/time) and student comfort
  • Maintain ratios per SSI standards; monitor breathing, buoyancy, and comfort

7. Post‑Dive Actions

  • Assist guest exit, debrief, congratulate
  • Log the dive in mySSI immediately (common step that is often skipped)
  • Explain BD recognition and continuing education options
  • Coordinate with Photo & Video team for media delivery (if purchased/included)
  • Settle remaining 50% payment if due

8. Site Selection Guidelines

  • Calm, shallow, sandy bottom preferred
  • Low current, good visibility
  • Simple entry/exit suitable for first‑timers

9. Safety Reminders

  • Abort if panic/equalization issues persist; safety and comfort over completion
  • Stay within no‑stop limits; follow environmental best practices
  • No guest‑operated cameras during the experience
