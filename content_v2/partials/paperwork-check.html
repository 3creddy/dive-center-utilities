How we verify paperwork (Dive India × SSI)

  1. Student mySSI App: Check that the student has completed the SSI digital paperwork for the relevant course in their mySSI app.
  2. SSI Backend (my.divessi.com):
    1. Login → Dive Center account.
    2. Open the student profile and confirm the course is assigned.
    3. Within the assigned course, open Documents / Paperwork and verify all required forms are completed and valid.

Note: No in‑water activities may begin until the above are verified. Keep a local record (training register entry) of who verified and when.
